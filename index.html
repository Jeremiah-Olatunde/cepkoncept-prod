about us

find out who we are and what we do

Cepkoncept LLC is a consultancy and development consortium (design and build outfit), incorporated in Nigeria. It is a limited liability company with aim of providing high quality of excellence in designs and quest for quality and functional developments or habitation at an affordable cost within short period.

CEO

dr. cephas adelore

Cephas Adeoye is the managing director of the company with over 25 years of experience. He has worked with many construction and consulting firms including Space & Place Limited, Spaceonline Limited and AMA Associates and attended many trade exhibitions both in Nigeria and abroad. Cephas Adelore is well known for his vast experience in the industry and his ability to work under pressure.
